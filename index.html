Jenny Nguyen is a first year at the University of California Riverside. She is involved a member of the Undergraduate Business Association and is part of their Events Committee that plans the organizations' bigger events. She is also a proud member of the Dean's Honor List at her university. Currently, Jenny is a pre-business student, but once she is in the School of Business, she hopes to concentrate in Management. As well as majoring in Business Management, she plans to double major in History and potentially minor in Classical Studies. Jenny would also like to study abroad during her time at the University of California, Riverside.

Jenny Nguyen attended Troy High School in Fullerton, California and graduated in 2019. In high school, she was a yearbook editor, robotics club board member, and FBLA competitor at her school. Jenny graduated with a 4.6 GPA and was a member of her school's National Honor Society, California Scholarship Federation, and Principal's Honors List. Jenny also volunteered throughout middle school and high school. She volunteered with the Summer Reading Program at the Placentia Public Library, which accumulated to over 100 hours of volunteer work. She has also worked with children within her church for the past six years by helping with the children's ministry. Every summer, she works as a small group leader during the Vacation Bible School Program. In this role, she leads children grades 3-6 through bible studies, games, and activities.

She currently works as a sales associate for Justice. At her job, she interacts with customers, processes shipments, and manages the cash register. She has worked at Justice for the past seven months and will continue to work there for the unforeseeable future. In the past, she interned with a non-profit called the Fullerton Collaborative. At this internship, she spent 6 weeks working on improving the non-profit's social media's presence, contacting members of the non-profit, and providing updates about current events around Fullerton relating to homelessness, youth empowerment, education, and health & wellness. Jenny currently resides in Fullerton, California with her family and their pets. In her free time, she enjoys listening to music, shopping, taking pictures, watching Netflix, and hanging out with her friends. Jenny also enjoys spending time with her family both in California and in North Carolina. She has a large family seeing as her mom is one of eight children and her father is one of nine. Considering the large size of her family, she is very close to with them.
